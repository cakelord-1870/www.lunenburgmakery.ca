OH CANADA Key Chain Felt Hand Sewing Kit

Regular price $8.00

Our felt craft kits use a high quality wool felt made in Europe to ensure the best durability of your new creation. All of the production stages of this kit - design, assembly, and packaging - have been lovingly completed by a small staff here in our little shop in Lunenburg, Nova Scotia.

Sew your very own maple leaf key chains! What better way to show off your love for Canada? Or Canadians. Or maple leaves. Or key chains.

This kit contains everything you will need to make a maple leaf key chain of your own. We have included the following in this kit:

  • Stitch tutorials and instructions
  • Blended wool felt
  • Paper pattern templates
  • Cotton embroidery floss
  • Sewing needle
  • Pins

Be sure to check out the rest of the kits in our collection!
